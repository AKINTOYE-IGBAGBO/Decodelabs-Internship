## PROJECT 2 - EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS

The objective of this project is to  analyse the dataset of 1200 E commerce sales analysis spanning accross 3 years from 2023 to 2025 to understand  dataset to understand  the patterns, trends and distributions.

### STEPS INVOLVED  IN EXPLORATORY ANALYSIS
1. Summary data analysis by calculating the basic statistics- mean, median and count.
2. Plotting the distribution graph of the total price.
3. Categorical data analaysis to check for trends and patterns.
4. Identifying the outliers.

 ### Tools
 1. Excel- Data analaysis for stastical data.
           Pivot table  for categorical data analysis.
           Interquartile range to find the out the outliers.

### Observations

##NUMERICAL DATA ANALYSIS
1. The distribution for the total price for the 1200 orders is a assymetrical distribution and right skewwed suggesting most orders are from modest revenue generating orders.
2. The high difference mean value between the mean value and median value suggest the presence of few large revenue generating orders.
3. Using the interquartile range, about 8 orders are the outliers formimg about 2 percent of the revenue and 1 percent of the total orders.



## CATEGORICAL DATA ANALYSIS
1. Among the product sales, the  printer is the most requested orders while chairs contribute to most of the revenue withnthe printer having a close call.
2. The highest orders came from instagram.
3. The most prefereable mode of payment is cash.
4. About 19 percent of orders are succesfully delivered without being cancelled or returned which a very low metric for business sustainability.
5. Majority of the orders are cancelled or returned.
6. Most cancelled orders are reffered from google, the most returned orderd are referred from facebook and the most delivererd orders came from email.
7. The highest cancelled product orders is the chairs, the highest returned product is the tablet and the highest delivered product is the laptop.
8. A high decline in the orders started from july.
9. The mean churn customers rate was in year 2025 leading to the least performing revenue year.
10. The very high percentage of cancelled orders and returned orders could tend to the loss of customers in the following year.
11. The high paying customers are lost across the years as about 25 percent of the orders were successfully deliverd in 2023.
